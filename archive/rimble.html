    • Rimble is a startup that uses machine learning models to generate real-time odds and analytics for eSports. I redesigned their website and helped establish their brand identity.

    Problem: Rimble's website was not useful or attractive to potential customers.

    Goal: Revamp site to act as a sales funnel, where customers can understand the product's value.

    Solution: A modern, product-focused redesign of the website.




    RESEARCH & IDEATION

    To get started, I explored the current website and looked at competitor sites.


    Current landing page before the redesign.



    I went through Rimble's current website, and wrote down potential areas of improvement. The team wanted me to work on the landing page, the about page, and the API documentation page.





    Then, I looked at other product-focused websites for startups in the space. These sites provided inspiration on how to structure my redesign.







    WIREFRAMING

    I began with rough sketches, and then transformed those sketches into wireframes on Figma.


    Lower fidelity designs of the landing page.






    FINAL DESIGN

    I created both the high-fidelity website prototype and visual design elements on Figma.
