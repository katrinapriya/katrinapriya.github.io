hey there, I'm Katrina! 👋


I am someone who cannot resist seeing the world through a design lens! Design thinking is how I approach problems, even while planning a party, organizing travel adventures, or completing a home project.

I majored in Data Science at UC Berkeley, while also earning certificates in entrepreneurship and design. My background helps me dig deeper into technical problem areas.

At Unqork, I've had the chance to craft the vision for some of the platform's trickiest technical problems. It has fueled my passion for breaking down complexity to create user-centric solutions.

I thrive on building a vibrant and supportive atmosphere within the teams I'm a part of. I find delight in celebrating wins and creative bonding events.

Outside of tech, I'm fascinated by the entertainment industry ― I actually had my TV debut recently on Law & Order: SVU! I also love to travel, draw, write, and dance.
