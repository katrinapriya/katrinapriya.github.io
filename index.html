Katrina Priya


Welcome to my page! I'm Katrina, a freshman at UC Berkeley studying computer science. I grew up in Michigan, but have been living in Los Angeles for the past three years. I'm interested in a career in technology and entrepreneurship.

I have experience with web and app development, having created Android & iOS mobile apps and this website. At Berkeley, I'm currently taking data structures and electrical engineering courses. I know Python, Java, C++, HTML, and have some mainframe experience.

I'm very passionate about encouraging more women to pursue computer science! In order to help diminish the gender gap in the male-dominated field of computer science, I've worked with middle school girls through workshops and clubs. I've been a part of PROJECTCSGIRLS and the #include Fellowship program. I hope to be a role model for females who are fearful of pursuing their computing passions, by sharing the opportunities that I have to those who lack it.

You can reach me at katrina.priya@berkeley.edu.
