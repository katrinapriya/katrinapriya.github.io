Pitch Perfect

August 2018, Udacity

This application records and plays back audio in different pitches. It's a part of Udacity's iOS Developer Nanodegree program, specifically created through the Intro to iOS App Development with Swift course. You can see my work on Github.



This was a nice refresher course on iOS app development. I became far more comfortable using Swift and Xcode through the creation of this application.
