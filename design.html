Trackin' Tag

Spring 2019, Product Design

After an extensive process of market research and brainstorming, my team and I designed an electronic bag tag.


This electronic bag tag gets rid of those long sticky paper tags we print at airports for checked-in luggage. The tag would contain an RFID chip so that it can be tracked after the flight. This would reduce lost luggage at airports. We also hope to implement an alert for when the bag arrives at baggage claim.

We used 3D printing to create this final mockup of our product!

BridgeBot

Summer 2019, Cisco CollabHacks

I'm designing a bot for Cisco Webex Teams that randomly groups people from the space in which the bot is called together. It encourages social interaction between different business units and roles, as well as being a tool for networking and mentorship.
It has some fun features like cuisine recommendations, compliments, and icebreakers.

It's still a work in progress, so I'll update as I finish more features!
