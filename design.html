Impactify

Present, StEP

I've been working on a team to create a platform that brings local impactful opportunities & education for social good to your fingertips. We hope for this platform to be a place where anyone can easily sign up for opportunties \& meet others interested in similar social issues! I am currently leading product design, but as any early-stage startup, we are all participating in every aspect of the product.


We developed this idea through our Social Entrepreneurship class and the StEP startup accelerator program. My team and I have gone through numerous users interviews, market research, mentoring sessions, and iterations of prototyping. We continue to evolve our idea to meet the needs of our users. We've been pitching at competitions and events in the Bay Area.

BridgeBot

Summer 2019, Cisco CollabHacks

I designed a bot for Cisco Webex Teams that randomly groups people from the space in which the bot is called together. It encourages social interaction between different business units and roles, as well as being a tool for networking and mentorship.
It has some fun features like cuisine recommendations, compliments, and icebreakers. My coworkers actually ended up using my bot even after my internship ended, which makes me very happy :)

Trackin' Tag

Spring 2019, Product Design

After an extensive process of market research and brainstorming, my team and I designed an electronic bag tag.


This electronic bag tag gets rid of those long sticky paper tags we print at airports for checked-in luggage. The tag would contain an RFID chip so that it can be tracked after the flight. This would reduce lost luggage at airports. We also hope to implement an alert for when the bag arrives at baggage claim.

We used 3D printing to create this final mockup of our product!
